## Hi, I'm Yecheng Zhang (张业成) 👋

![Visitors](https://komarev.com/ghpvc/?username=24kchengYe&color=blue&style=flat-square)

PhD Student at Tsinghua University.

My research lies at the intersection of **LLM/VLM Evaluation & Alignment**, **Multi-Agent Systems**, and **Urban AI**. I design benchmarks and training-free alignment methods for large generative models, and build global-scale urban datasets. Passionate about building AI productivity tools — currently working on [Recall](https://github.com/24kchengYe/claude-code-recall) and [DigitalMe](https://github.com/24kchengYe/digitalme).
