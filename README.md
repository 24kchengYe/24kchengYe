## Hi, I'm Yecheng Zhang (张业成) 👋

PhD Student at [Beijing City Lab](https://www.beijingcitylab.com/), Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. [Ying Long](https://www.arch.tsinghua.edu.cn/info/rw_yjsjy/1634).

### Research

My research lies at the intersection of **LLM/VLM Evaluation & Alignment**, **Multi-Agent Systems**, and **Urban AI**. I design benchmarks and training-free alignment methods for large generative models, and build global-scale urban datasets.

**Key works:**

- **AI4US** — A benchmark framework evaluating LLMs on scientific data synthesis across symbolic reasoning and multimodal perception (10,000+ experiments). *Nature Cities* (2nd round review).
- **UrbanAlign** — Training-free post-hoc VLM-human preference alignment via multi-agent reasoning (+28.8pp accuracy over raw VLM). *ECCV 2026* (under review).
- **CMAB** — China's Multi-Attribute Building Dataset covering 32M+ buildings (23,000+ downloads, **ESI Highly Cited**). *Scientific Data*, 2025.
- **GloGCI** — Global Ghost City Index across 10,000+ cities. Featured by [Popular Mechanics](https://www.popularmechanics.com/). *Habitat International*, 2025.
